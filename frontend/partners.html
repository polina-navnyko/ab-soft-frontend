Partners

We are commited to working with industry leaders to provide our customers with world-class technology solutions.

1Vision LTD


1Vision is a group of highly experienced system integrators and application developers, specializing in providing imaging & machine vision products and solutions to the Israeli market. 1Vision is an authorized distributor of A&B Software products in Israel.

Allied Vision Technologies


Allied Vision Technologies designs, produces and sells cameras and components for image processing in industrial and life science applications. With innovative products, superior manufacturing quality and a service-driven organization, AVT is well established as a premier provider of digital camera solutions for machine vision worldwide. A&B Software partners with AVT to provide complete 1394 and GigE Vision camera based imaging solutions to software developers and end-users.

Alrad Imaging


Alrad is a provider of high quality scientific equipment to industry and research. Through its two trading divisions, Alrad Imaging and Electronics, it offers thousands of product items from the world's leading manufacturers. Alrad Imaging is an authorized distributor of A&B Software products in the British Isles.

Basler Vision Technologies


Basler-VC designs and manufactures color and monochrome line scan, area scan, and intelligent digital cameras ideal for high-speed and/or high-resolution applications. Basler and A&B Software work together in promoting and supporting IIDC-1394 and GigE Vision standards.

BitFlow Inc


BitFlow is a Massachusetts based producer of analog, digital and Camera Link framegrabbers for the imaging industry. BitFlow and A&B Software partner in developing and supporting ImageWarp and ActiveBF.

Datapath Limited


Datapath is a world leading innovator in the field of video graphics, video capture and video wall display technology. A&B Software's ImageWarp product provides a high-performance interface to all Datapath's capture cards.
